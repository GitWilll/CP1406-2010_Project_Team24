The Townsville Community Music Centre is a not-for-profit organisation. The TCMC presents concerts and workshops throughout the year. It is home to a diverse range of genres, including; classical, jazz, folk, blues, world and contemporary music! Our concerts feature touring artists, as well as locally-based professional and emerging artists.

A bit about us:

The Townsville Community Music Centre has been a major part of the musical culture of Townsville for many years, hosting a number of musical events in the region. The TCMC is a not-for-profit organisation, which relies on the generosity of it's members and volunteers to keep us going. We are always looking for new people to join in and voluteer. So if you love music, would like to meet some great people and enjoy a good time, please apply today!

The TCMC supports local talents, performances from a number of great artists including: Harbourside Duo, Aviva Quartet, Celtic Fyre, THe Dirty Dozen, Aquapella, Townsvilles own Concert band, Wassa and many more!

Live House Music:

TCMC are proud organisers of the Live House Music Service, which sees student musicians perform infront of a live audience at the Townsville Civic Theatre, before concerts. If you are part of a small instrumental group and would like the oppertunity to perform infront of a live audience before a gig, contact the TCMC to apply!

If you are a performer and would like to play with us, we will produce and promote your performance for you! Please, don't hesitate to contact us for information.
